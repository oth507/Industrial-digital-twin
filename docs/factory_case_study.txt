
Case Study: Digital Twin Energy Optimization in a Manufacturing Facility

Industry: Steel Processing Plant

Factory Overview:
The factory operates several heavy industrial machines including furnaces,
compressors, and rolling equipment. Energy consumption represents nearly
35% of the operational cost of the facility.

Problem:
Energy consumption peaks during mid‑day production cycles due to inefficient
machine scheduling and idle running equipment.

Proposed Solution:
A Digital Twin system is implemented to simulate machine behavior and monitor
energy consumption in real time.

System Components:
• IoT sensors measuring electricity consumption and machine load
• Data collection platform
• Digital Twin simulation model
• AI‑based optimization algorithm

Results from Simulation:
Baseline daily energy consumption: 480 kWh
Optimized daily consumption: 402 kWh

Energy reduction: 16%
Annual energy savings: ~28,000 kWh
Estimated CO2 reduction: ~12 tons per year

Conclusion:
The case study demonstrates that Digital Twin‑based energy optimization can
significantly improve industrial efficiency and sustainability.
